A pair of narrow, historic streets in the Vinegar Hill neighborhood of Brooklyn.

History

Little Street and Evans Street are remnants of the 19th-century development of Vinegar Hill, originally an industrial area. The streets are known for their cobblestone surfaces and preserved row houses, offering a glimpse into Brooklyn's past.
