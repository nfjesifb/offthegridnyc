Mott Haven Water Front

← Back to Main Map

Description: A revitalized waterfront area in the Mott Haven neighborhood of the Bronx, featuring parks and recreational spaces.

History: The Mott Haven waterfront underwent significant redevelopment in the early 21st century, transforming from an industrial zone into a public space with parks, walkways, and access to the Harlem River. This project is part of broader efforts to improve public access to waterfronts across New York City.
