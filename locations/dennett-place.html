Dennett Place

Description

A small, historic street in Carroll Gardens, Brooklyn, known for its charming row houses.

History

Dennett Place was developed in the mid-19th century as part of the expansion of Carroll Gardens. The street is notable for its well-preserved Italianate and Greek Revival-style homes, reflecting the neighborhood's architectural heritage.
