Off The Grid NYC

Off the Grid NYC is a project documenting the most beautiful, unique, and often hidden reminents of good urbanism that remain in New York City. Each of these places are interesting for different reasons some involved inovative solutions to communal space, noise polution, or safety. But every building, street, home, or community here has in common that they represent a small piece of the city that lives in its own world or era, "Off the Grid".
